Who am I?

I’m a first year MBA student at the Georgetown McDonough School of Business, with over 6 years of experience working at the intersection of social impact and technology.

I've spent my career working across microfinance, EdTech, rural livelihoods, and with governments and NGO's to understand the layout of the social impact space. During this time, I worked as a product manager of solutions aimed at serving marginalized communities. I plan to use my STEM MBA and to work with market leaders to further explore how technology can drive change in emerging markets.
